About iKamAI

Learn more about our mission to create an inclusive world through technology.

Breaking Down Communication Barriers

iKamAI is a revolutionary AI-powered platform developed to bridge the communication gap between the deaf community and the hearing world. Our name "iKamAI" combines "Kamay" (Filipino for "Hand") with AI, reflecting our mission to use artificial intelligence to interpret hand gestures.

Founded with a vision to create a more inclusive society, our team of experts has developed advanced algorithms that can accurately recognize and translate sign language in real-time.

  • Accessible for all users regardless of technical expertise
  • Continually improving through machine learning
  • Real-time translation with minimal latency
